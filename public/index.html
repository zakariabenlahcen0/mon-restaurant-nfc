Julianna's Restaurant opened in 1989, and through the years has grown into a Kalamazoo favorite for the whole family! With delicious offerings of home-style breakfast and lunches, Julianna's is recognized as one of the finest restaurants in Southwest Michigan. We are locally owned and operated, and we take pride in offering every customer the personal attention they deserve. We make special orders, so even if you don't see something on the menu be sure to ask! Our goal is to provide top-quality home-style meals at a reasonable price in a casual environment. We operate our restaurants with integrity and respect, and we do it in a personal way! For us, it will always be about combining fresh and indulgent flavors to fit everyone's mood! Whether you live in the area or are just passing through, Julianna's is your ultimate Southwest Michigan breakfast and lunch location!
